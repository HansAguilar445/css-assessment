Welcome

Purpose of the Website

This is a website about the Kirby series, specifically about its history and lore. The Kirby series, despite its looks as just a cute and friendly series, has some surprisingly dark and deep lore within its pause menus. From the existence of the Ancients who were responsible for much of the technology within the series to things like the Clockwork Stars such as Galactic Nova from Kirby Super Star's Milky Way Wishes mode and the Access Ark from Kirby Planet Robobot, the Kirby series has far more interesting lore than you would think from looking at it. This website will cover these things as well as the history of the series in its development.

Other Things

Besides the Kirby history and lore, there is further content related to Kirby that will be covered here like challenge videos and videos also covering the lore of the series.
